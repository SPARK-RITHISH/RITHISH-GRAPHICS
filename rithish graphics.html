The Benefits of Using Grid-Based Designs Grid-based design is a powerful tool for creating visually compelling layouts. With its use, designers can create appealing and consistent designs quickly and easily. It helps define important hierarchy in visual elements on the page or screen, so users know where to focus their attention first. Not only does grid-based design bring structure to content but it offers an easy way to get creative by allowing you to experiment with different visuals within a perfectly measured framework.
